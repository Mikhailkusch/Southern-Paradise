II. Approach

Photography leads. Everything else stays out of the way.

— On the work

Southern Paradise was started for one reason: too many extraordinary safari operations were being represented online by content that didn't match the experience on the ground.

We work with a small portfolio of camps and lodges across Zambia, Botswana, and South Africa. We handle strategy, on-property production, monthly content cycles, and the reporting that keeps owners and operators close to what's actually happening.

01

Strategy

Audience positioning, content pillars, and a calendar that moves with the seasons of your camp.

02

Production

On-property photography and video — guides, wildlife, food, golden hour. Edited to a single editorial standard.

03

Reporting

A monthly long-form report. Performance, demographics, what worked and what's next. Written, not auto-generated.
